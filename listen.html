About us: Smackgirls

Kanoko and Lisa are marked by an unmistakable synergy on (and off) stage. Surrendering to each other’s gravity, within a short period of time their collaboration has been marked by a strong sense of kinship, sisterhood, mutual understanding, and most and foremost miracle. There is a great amount of energy, love and admiration for each other’s work flowing and circulating between the two. This connection of theirs has become tremendously precious to them.

In 2023 they have performed in Het Concertgebouw during the Final of the Goodmesh Concours, and have been awarded the 1st Prize.

The Dokwerk Saxophone Quartet is a young and energetic chamber music ensemble established in 2019 and based in Amsterdam. In combining their four different cultures and backgrounds they are provided a never-ceasing richness and variety of musical possibilities and choices.
