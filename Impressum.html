Cookies
This website makes use of ‘cookies’ (text files that are placed on your computer) for the purpose of analyzing user activity on the website. These are exclusively analytical and functional in nature. We use this information to monitor the way in which you use our website, to generate reports on website usage and to offer other services that relate to website activity and internet usage. The default settings on most browsers are such that cookies are automatically accepted; however, you can change your browser settings to reject all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is being sent. Please note that it is possible that some of the website’s functions and services may not work properly if your browser is set to automatically reject all cookies.
